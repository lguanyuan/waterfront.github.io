Woodlands Waterfront Park


Woodlands Waterfront Park is a park located at Admiralty Road West in Singapore and overlooks the sea from the north. Is is officially opened in January 2011. The theme of this park is Active and Healthy Community Lifestyle. It is considerably spacious with an area of approximately 10-hectares. featuring a 1.5KM long waterfront promenade, 400 meters jetty (along with a seafood restaurant at the mid-section of the jetty) and a multi-generation playground. The 6-hactares of undulating terrain within the coastal park also appeals to jogging and rollerskating enthusiasts. Behind the carpark area and West of the entrance is Picnic Green, which is an open field kept shady by the surrounding trees. This is the spot specifically designed for you to set up your picnic as it is not as hilly compared to other areas of the park. You will not find many people on this side of the park as most of the crowd would divert to either the jetty or the playground..

How to get there?

1. By Bus: Take Bus Service 856 from Woodlands or Yishun Interchange ( Woodlands is prefered as it takes less stops to reach) and alight at "Bef W'Lands Waterfront Pk" Bus stop ID: 47011

2. By Car: Address is Admiralty Rd W, Singapore 759956

Places of attraction


Here are some of the attractions of Woodlands Waterfront Park coupled with the activities that can be done

1.Jetty


The jetty is good for an evening stroll and you can see many families with young tots in strollers along its length.
If you have the patience you can even get ready your fishing gear and aim for a good catch.

You can also get a good view of the straits of Johor from the jetty and enjoy the view of the sunset.

2.Scenic trail

The Scenic Trail can also be found on the East side of the park where the trail is the longest and straightest, made specially for runners, cyclists and the likes. There is a small football field not far from the playground if you follow the path towards the Scenic Trail. Woodlands Waterfront Park is a good place for cyclists of all experience as it has a variety of tracks from steep and gentle slopes, to paths with strong curvature or you can choose to enjoy a leisure cycle on a straight path and enjoy the breeze.

3.Playground


Woodlands Waterfront Park features a multi-dimensional playground. The equipment here is suitable for all ages from toddlers to the eldery so it is the perfect place for a family day out to bond with each other! The centrepiece of the playground is the two-storey-high Sky Walk a series of six crows nets that are interlinked by mesh bridges which people can climb and cross

View of the playground in the evening with the sunset



The playground can definitely entertain and occupy many children for a good hour or more.

4.Rasa Istimewa Waterfront Restaurant


1. If you are planning to come here on an empty stomach and may worry about the inaccessibility to any food outlet, fret not as Rasa Istimewa Waterfront Restaurant is located right on the jetty and it is a full Halal restaurant.
2.The opening hours are on Sundays to Thursdays: 2 pm till midnight, Fridays and Saturdays: 3 pm till 1 am.
3. There are plenty of high chairs for the little ones and lots of space within the airy eatery.

Disclaimer: Our vegetarians friends do have to take note that there are no vegetarian options here and thus there will not be any food outlet for you guys.

5. Central Spine


Straight ahead from the entrance of Woodlands Waterfront is the Central Spine Event Plaza, a two story complex outfitted with toilets, water coolers and vending machines. This is also one of the largest shelter around the park to seek refuge should it start to rain. It can also be an ideal place for a small scale event like a birthday celebration or just a simple gathering point for less than 20 pax.

Side activities


1. Picnic


You can choose a cosy spot to set up a picnic, be it on the jetty, along the pathway,nearby the playground or even in those sheltered resting spots. Having a picnic is a great way to pass time at the park with a great evening view to match.

2. Kite flying


Kites flying is a favourite activity at the park as there are no trees to enshare your precious kites if you stay away from the fringe of the sloping opened area and the costal breeze is also a plus point.

3.Photography

There is definitely something for shutterbugs at this park, be it the scenic views of the Johor Straits, gorgeous sunrise/sunset glow, or flora and fauna.

Admiralty Park (and Republic Polytechnic) is linked to the Waterfront, so you can hop over to see what

Admiralty Park has to offer if you still want more fun.

Conclusion


Generally, I feel that Woodlands Waterfront Park is a must go as a Singaporean especially if you stay in the North region of Singapore. The park boast a plethora of facilites that makes it worth your trip down. It is suitable for different occasions such as a family day out, celebrating a friend's birthday, a casual gathering and even for a date! So if you are looking for an outdoor venue to relax and have fun, Woodlands Waterfront is one place that you can consider.
Fun fact: Woodlands Waterfront is a good area for PokemonGoers with 2 gyms and 5 pokestops located around Central Spine, so what are you waiting for? Gotta catch them all!
